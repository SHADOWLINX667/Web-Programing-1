Introduction

Devil May Cry is an action game series by Capcom. You play as demon hunters and fight enemies using swords, guns, and special powers.

The series is famous for its fast, stylish combat. The goal is not just to win — it is to win with style.

  • First game came out in 2001.
  • The main character is Dante, a half-human half-demon.
  • There are 5 main games in the series.
Characters

Each character has a different playstyle. Here are the main ones:

  • Dante — The main hero. Uses swords and guns. Cocky and funny.
  • Vergil — Dante's twin brother. Serious and powerful. Uses a katana.
  • Nero — A younger hunter with a mechanical arm.
  • V — Fights using summoned demons instead of weapons.
Combat

Combat is about chaining different moves together without getting hit. The more variety you use, the higher your style rank goes.

  • Mix melee attacks with gunshots to keep combos going.
  • Repeating the same move lowers your rank.
  • Getting hit also lowers your rank.
  • Devil Trigger is a special power boost you can activate mid-fight.
Games

There are five main games in the series. Here they are in order:

  • Devil May Cry (2001) — The original game.
  • Devil May Cry 2 (2003) — The weakest entry.
  • Devil May Cry 3 (2005) — Fan favourite. Tells Dante's origin story.
  • Devil May Cry 4 (2008) — Introduced Nero.
  • Devil May Cry 5 (2019) — The latest and best starting point.
Style Ranks

After every fight, the game grades your performance from D up to SSS.

  • D — Dull
  • C — Cruel
  • B — Brutal
  • A — Atomic
  • S — Smokin' Sexy Style
  • SS — Smokin' Sick Style
  • SSS — Smokin' Stylish Showstopper
Beginner Tips

The game can feel hard at first. These tips will help:

  • Start on Devil Hunter difficulty, not Human.
  • Don't spam one button — mix up your attacks.
  • Dodge a lot. Getting hit is bad for your rank.
  • Spend red orbs on new moves as early as possible.
  • Watch the style meter in the top left during fights.
